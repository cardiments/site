Cardiments is the brainchild of a UK drummer Jack Hanks. Jack decided to get good at drums using rudients. Not being the brightest penny in the jar he found drumming text books and full score notation a bit daunting.

Jack decided to make his own flash cards to memorise rudiments. Realising the 26 standard rudiments published by NARD fitted on 2 suits in a deck of playing cards this got him thinking

One thing led to another and several months later he had developed with help from Brian Bestall, Jo Rucklidge, Liz Hanks, Jay Leach, Casey Donovan, Dan Drums. Justin Lewis, Leo Nicholson, Kong Stevie and Rakel
