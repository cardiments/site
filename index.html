A great companion for drummers on a mission to play like the greats. Made by drummers to learn vocabulary, build solos and develop technique.

Welcome to the cardiments website. This site is designed as a companion to the cardiments playing card deck.

You will find lots of resources here to help your development as a rudimental drummer.

Over the coming weeks this website will grow. The will be updates in products and news as well as an expanded help section. Please bookmark and keep visiting for some nice surprises in the near future.

About

Cardiments id the brainchild of a UK drummer Jack. Jack decided to get good at drums using rudiments. He found drumming text books and full score notation a bit daunting.

Jack decided to make his own flash cards to memorise rudiments. Realising the 26 standard rudiments published by NARD fitted on 2 suits in a deck of playing cards this got him thinking One thing led to another and several months later cardiments was born!
