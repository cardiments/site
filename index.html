Rudiments on cards!

From Single Stroke Rolls to Triple Ratamacues.

A great companion for drummers on a mission to play like the greats. Made by drummers to learn vocabulary, build solos and develop technique.

Welcome to the cardiments website. This site is designed as a companion to the cardiments playing card deck.

You will find lots of resources here to help your development as a rudimental drummer.

About

Cardiments was created by UK drummer Jack, who decided to practice drums using rudiments and created special cards to memorise them.

Realising the 26 standard rudiments published by NARD fitted on 2 suits in a deck of playing cards; this got him thinking. One thing led to another and before long Cardiments was born!
