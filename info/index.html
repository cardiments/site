Rudiments are set pattern of notes played between 2 hands on a drum. They can also be applied to a drum set.

Rudiments first appeared on the battlefields of medieval Europe. They were used to to relay orders. Different armies had different rudiments with different meanings.

Rudiments were brought to the U.S.A. evolving into a musical form. A group of drummers known as N.A.R.D published the "Standard 26 American Drum Rudiments" in Chicago - 1933.
