Notation :: The rudiments in this deck have been notated in a simple easy way to read. There are other notations for other purposes *IMG PcSt* Variations :: There are endless ways to play and combine these rudiments. How they are phrased and combined define a drummers sound *IMG PcSt* Sticking :: The notation shows R Dominant L Non-dominant hands. Southpaws can switch these stickings. All rudiments should be played and practiced both ways round
